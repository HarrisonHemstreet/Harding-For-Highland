Intro

Highland is a unique city. Planning Our Future Together we can keep it that way.

My husband and I have been married for 38 years. We have 5 married sons and 10 wonderful grandchildren. We love living in the beautiful city of Highland because of its small town feel with its parks, trails, and scenic views.

I earned my degree in International Relations from BYU. I’ve taught piano for more than 25 years. My ability to lead has been cultivated by my life experiences. Living in many cities throughout the United States and overseas, I have seen how good policy can help a community and how bad can hurt. I have served the youth and women of my church in many leadership positions as well as my community at my children’s schools. My continued desire to serve led me to attend Highland City Council Meetings where I have listened to and learned about the challenges facing Highland today. Water, open space, and zoning are just a few of these challenges. I am committed to addressing these issues and listening to your views. We can achieve our goals by Planning Our Future Together.

Issues

  • Water: Let’s do our part to conserve.
  • Open Space: Each neighborhood is unique. Let’s put policies in place that will be equitable to all.
  • Zoning: Let’s develop with Highland’s General Plan in mind. (low density) Let’s keep residential roads safe.

I Promise to:

  • Be fiscally responsible and plan for the future.
  • Listen to and communicate with residents.
  • Do my homework on the issues to be prepared for City Council.
